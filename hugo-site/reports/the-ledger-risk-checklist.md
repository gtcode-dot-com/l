# The Ledger — Legal/Editorial Risk Checklist

| Flagged sentence (location) | Risk | Safer rewrite (if needed) |
|---|---|---|
| “Schedule I … indicates records were not maintained for grants (self‑reported).” (Executive Summary; Findings §3) | Could be read as an allegation of improper recordkeeping. | “Schedule I lists the records‑maintained indicator as ‘No’ (self‑reported); this entry does not, by itself, establish noncompliance.” |

| “One‑year anomaly” (Findings §2) | Can sound accusatory. | “One‑year spike relative to FY2021–FY2023.” |

Residual risk is low after revisions; remaining items are mitigated by explicit attribution and “self‑reported” qualifiers.
