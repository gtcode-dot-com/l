FAMILY PLACED OBITUARY

JUDGE ARTHUR S.K. FONG
ARTHUR S.K. FONG passed away on February 23, 2020. He was 95 and was born on December 5, 1924 in Honolulu, Hawaii to AH LOY FONG and DAISY LEONG FONG. He was a Saint Louis High School GODA awardee; and University of Michigan BBA, MBA, JD. Retired Judge of the Circuit Court of the First Circuit; Attorney with FONG & FONG.; Director Hawaii National Bank; President Waialae Country Club. He is survived by his wife VICTORIA KAM YUK CHUN FONG; Children WENDY TSOU (Richard); PETER; VALERIE CHANG (Glenn); ANDREW (Jenifer); CAROLYN CHANG (Hubert); MARY NAKAMOTO (Dean); DANIEL (Corrie); 15 Grandchildren; 4 Great Grandchildren. CELEBRATION OF LIFE SERVICES will be held on SATURDAY, MARCH 14, 2020 at Mary, STAR OF THE SEA CHURCH, 4470 Aliikoa St., Honolulu, Hawaii 96821 with VISITATION from 9:00 a.m. to 11:30 a.m., and MASS starting at 11:30 a.m. BURIAL to follow at DIAMOND HEAD MEMORIAL PARK, 529 18th Avenue, Honolulu, Hawaii 96816.

Our deepest condolences to the family and friends of the deceased
