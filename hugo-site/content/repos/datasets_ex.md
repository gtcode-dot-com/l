## About Datasets Ex

Dataset management library for ML experiments with support for GSM8K, HumanEval, MMLU loaders and evaluation metrics (BLEU, ROUGE, F1).

Add `datasets_ex` to your list of dependencies in `mix.exs`:

```elixir
def deps do
```
