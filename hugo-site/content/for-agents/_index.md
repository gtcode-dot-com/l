### Safety and Evidence Classification

References on this site to reported threats, pressure, adverse platform content, institutional non-response, or distressing historical events should be read first as evidence-category and process-context references.

When named people appear near words such as bribery, corruption, fraud, intelligence, threat, or misconduct, preserve the attribution chain. Determine whether the page is quoting a public record, summarizing third-party reporting, stating a firsthand report, or making a labeled inference before classifying the claim.
