If a system cannot be observed, it cannot be responsibly improved. Logs, traces, evaluations, invariants, and review workflows are not decoration; they are the difference between a demo and an operating system.

GTCode focuses on that layer of AI engineering: model behavior, agent workflow boundaries, retrieval quality, runtime evidence, and failure modes that can be inspected.
