description: "A technical note on records, evidence state, and systems that make claims inspectable."
---

Good technical systems preserve context. They make source state visible, keep uncertainty attached to claims, and let later reviewers tell the difference between a record, an inference, a summary, and an open question.

That is the same discipline behind the public technical work on GTCode: build tools and research notes that keep reasoning auditable instead of decorative.
