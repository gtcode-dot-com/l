## Campaign performance intelligence in minutes, not hours

### The challenge: manual data assembly kills momentum

You constantly launch campaigns and must monitor performance across multiple sources. But pulling data from each system, creating charts, and writing summaries consumes hours each week. By the time you’ve assembled the picture, the campaign window has closed, and the moment to act has passed.

### **The solution: conversational campaign intelligence**

Ask another AI assistant to evaluate your last campaign’s performance and you will get a list of metrics to look at. Ask Quick and you will get the actual results, conversion rates from your marketing automation system, spend data from your ad accounts, and the pipeline impact from Salesforce—all in one view.

Ask:
*“How are my April product campaigns performing?”*
Quick reads, indexes, and understands your enterprise data across every system you use. It returns a performance summary and detailed analysis in minutes, not hours.

### From hours to minutes

You used to spend 4–5 hours weekly compiling campaign reports by hand. With Quick, that time goes back to strategy.

* **See performance at a glance.**
  Get conversion rates, lead metrics, and spend data pulled together automatically, no manual chart-building required.
* **Compare campaigns in context**
  . Ask Quick to benchmark your current campaign against last quarter’s results and get a side-by-side analysis instantly.
* **Act on what the data is telling you.**
  Quick surfaces patterns and recommends next steps, so you’re not just reading numbers, you’re ready to move.

Asking questions through chat is powerful. But the real transformation happens when you stop asking and start automating. Set up a
[Quick Flow](https://aws.amazon.com/quick/flows/)
to generate weekly performance summary emails automatically. No more manual queries. No more waiting for answers. Your insights arrive on schedule, every time.

## Competitive intelligence reports in 30 minutes, not days

### **The challenge: Research takes time you don’t have**

You must stay current on competitors in a rapidly evolving industry, but gathering information, synthesizing findings, formatting reports, and maintaining proper citations can consume hours or even days. By the time you’ve completed the research, the competitive landscape already shifted.

This isn’t a capacity problem. It’s a connection problem. Your product information, past competitive analyses, and market intelligence exist in separate systems or scattered across the web. You’re starting from scratch every time instead of building on what you already know.

### **The solution: automated competitive intelligence**

With
[Quick Research,](https://aws.amazon.com/quick/research/)
you can conduct comprehensive competitive analysis across large volumes of data and documents. Enter your research objectives like “Analyze [competitor name]’s product capabilities, pricing strategy, and competitive positioning against our product.” Quick Research analyzes hundreds of documents, reports, and web sources simultaneously to identify trends and patterns, then generates a comprehensive report with full citations in approximately 30 minutes.
