## Providing new places to generate music

High-quality music generation should be accessible wherever creativity happens. Whether you are an app developer, a business or music professional, or a creator, these integrations allow you to use Lyria’s advanced musical awareness to scale your production.

[**Vertex AI**](https://cloud.google.com/vertex-ai)
**:**
Lyria 3 Pro is now in public preview on Vertex AI for businesses who require on-demand audio at scale. It gives organizations the ability to scale high-fidelity production, from rapidly generating bespoke soundtracks for gaming to integrating into creative tools, music and video platforms.

[**Google AI Studio**](https://aistudio.google.com/)
**and the**
[**Gemini API**](https://ai.google.dev/gemini-api/docs)
**:**
For
[developers](https://blog.google/innovation-and-ai/technology/developers-tools/lyria-3-developers/)
building the next generation of creative tools, Lyria 3 provides improved musical awareness and structural coherence to offer creative flexibility. Lyria 3 Pro is now available alongside
[Lyria RealTime](https://ai.google.dev/gemini-api/docs/realtime-music-generation)
in AI Studio.

[**Google Vids**](https://docs.google.com/videos/create?usp=blog)
**:**
Vids is an AI-powered video creation app that anyone can use. With Lyria 3 and Lyria 3 Pro in Vids, you can add custom music that matches your style for everything from creative projects to marketing videos. This is rolling out to Google Workspace
[customers](https://support.google.com/docs/answer/15609411?hl=en)
and Google AI Pro & Ultra
[subscribers](https://gemini.google/subscriptions/)
starting this week.

[**Gemini app**](http://gemini.google.com/music)
**:**
Longer generations with Lyria 3 Pro are now available in the Gemini app, starting with paid subscribers. Lyria 3 Pro’s enhanced customization offers more space to experiment and play with longer tracks. So now, you can add more details to bring your full vision to life, or create personalized tracks for vlogs, podcasts or tutorial videos.

[**ProducerAI**](https://producer.ai/)
**:**
We recently introduced
[ProducerAI](https://blog.google/innovation-and-ai/models-and-research/google-labs/producerai/)
, a collaborative music creation tool, built by musicians looking for new ways to enhance their creative process. With Lyria 3 Pro, ProducerAI offers an agentic experience designed to help artists, producers and songwriters at every level iterate on comprehensive songs. It’s available globally to free and paid subscribers.
