DW Akademie is looking for a Project Officer for its EU-funded project SUSTAIN MEDIA – Support to Media Freedom and Pluralism in the Western Balkan. The project forms part of a bigger EU funded project, which is led by the Deutsche Gesellschaft für Internationale Zusammenarbeit (GIZ) as lead implementor. DW Akademie is responsible for the consulting services and trainings for media partners. The project aims to strengthen media outlets in the six Western Balkan countries through capacity building in business models, audience development, and innovative formats.
