## Upcoming Speaking Engagements

This is a current list of where and when I am scheduled to speak:

* I’m giving a keynote at
  [Cybernation 2026](https://wirtschaftsrat.de/de/hoehepunktveranstaltungen/wir-bauen-die-cybernation-von-der-vision-zur-wirkung/)
  in Berlin, Germany, on June 24, 2026.
* I’m speaking at the
  [Potsdam Conference on National Cybersecurity](https://potsdamer-sicherheitskonferenz.de/)
  at the Hasso Plattner Institut in Potsdam, Germany. The event runs June 24–25, 2026, and my talk will be the evening of June 24.
* I’m participating in a panel discussion at the
  [Austrian Institute for International Affairs](https://www.oiip.ac.at/en/events/disruption-or-singularity-ai-security-and-governance-in-the-21st-century/)
  in Vienna on Thursday, June 25, 2026.
* I’m speaking at the
  [Digital Humanism Conference](https://dighum.wien/)
  in Vienna, Austria, on Friday, June 26, 2026.
* I’m giving a fireside chat for Epicenter Works, to be held at
  [Kaffee Alt Wien](https://pretix.eu/epicenter-works/Fireside-Chat-Bruce-Schneier/)
  in Vienna, Austria, on Friday, June 26, 2026.
* I’m participating (via Zoom) in a panel discussion at
  [Quantum.Tech World](https://www.alphaevents.com/events-quantumtechus/)
  in Boston, Massachusetts, USA, on Friday, June 26, 2026. The topic is “Q-Day’s Shortening Deadline: Immediate Solutions.”
* I’m speaking at
  [Czech Technical University](https://akce.cvut.cz/?node=cal_item&amp;idevent=10047&amp;lang=en)
  in Prague, Czechia, on Monday, June 29, 2026.
* I’m speaking at the
  [Nuremberg Digital Festival](https://nuernberg.digital/de/)
  in Nuremburg, Germany, on Wednesday, July 1, 2026.
* I’m speaking at
  [CanSecWest 2026](https://www.secwest.net/)
  in Vancouver, Canada. The conference runs September 30–October 1, 2026; the time of my talk is TBD.

The list is maintained on
[this page](https://www.schneier.com/events/)
