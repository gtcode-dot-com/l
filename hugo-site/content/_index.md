## Our Mission

### Independent Investigative Journalism for Hawaii

Oahu Underground exists for one reason: to hold power accountable in Hawaii. We are an independent, Hawaii-based investigative newsroom — no corporate owners, no political affiliations, no editorial interference.

Our reporting draws on years of ground-level research into institutional accountability failure, judicial conduct, and the mechanisms that can shield misconduct from public view. Public-record claims are sourced to documentation. Firsthand observations and sealed-record-dependent claims are labeled as such.
