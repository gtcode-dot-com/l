# McKinley Incident History

**Prepared by:** Paul Edward Lowndes
**Purpose:** Personal master chronology and background reference regarding incidents involving Jason Dean McKinley. This document is intended primarily for personal records and as a source document for later use in court filings, attorney consultation, law-enforcement contacts, or agency submissions. It is not intended to be the primary DOJ submission.

## Parties

**Reporting party:** Paul Edward Lowndes
**Other party:** Jason Dean McKinley, Waialua, Oʻahu, Hawaiʻi

## Background

In approximately September 2021, I terminated a short work and personal relationship with Jason Dean McKinley. In November 2021, I attempted to collect a $200 debt for work I had performed. Later that same month, after a physical incident at Starbucks in Haleʻiwa, I informed McKinley in writing that I was writing off the debt and would not pursue further contact or in-person interaction. I state that after that point I did not attempt to locate or intercept him and that later in-person encounters were coincidental and occurred while I was on foot or bicycling in public areas.

## Incident Summary

### 1. November 2021 — Physical assault at Starbucks, Haleʻiwa

While I was seated outside Starbucks in Haleʻiwa, McKinley allegedly kicked me in the leg as he exited the location. I experienced physical pain that lasted about a day and felt fear and stress afterward. After the incident, McKinley went toward his vehicle and left.

### 2. November 2021 — Follow-up written cutoff

Later the same day, I emailed McKinley stating that I was writing off the $200 dispute, would not pursue further contact, and wanted no further in-person interaction. I regarded that message as the end of any effort to collect the money.

### 3. December 2021 — Vehicular intimidation, Malama Marketplace, Haleʻiwa

While I was on foot near Malama Marketplace, McKinley allegedly drove into the lot at speed, arced his truck toward me, and came within roughly two feet of me. He allegedly yelled, “stop stalking me,” and drove away. About an hour later I encountered him again on foot, and he repeated the same accusation. I called 911 but did not file a formal report at that time.

### 4. June 2022 — Vehicular threat, outside Breakers / North Shore Marketplace, Haleʻiwa

While I was walking south on the shoulder, McKinley allegedly drove his Nissan Frontier toward me, pointed it directly at me, accelerated in my direction, and then braked hard just short of me. I called 911 and reported the incident.

### 5. July 2022, approximately 7:00 a.m. — Dangerous vehicle pass / cut-off, Waialua Beach Road at Kaimanu Place

While I was bicycling east on the shoulder, McKinley allegedly passed me very closely from behind at high speed and then cut onto the shoulder directly in front of me, with the corner of his vehicle passing within about a foot of me. I called 911 and reported the incident.

### 6. July 24, 2022 — Cease-and-desist email

I sent McKinley a cease-and-desist email. I retained a copy.

### 7. September 12, 2022, about 9:50 a.m. — Vehicular threat, PaaLaa Road, Haleʻiwa

While I was walking east on the shoulder of PaaLaa Road, McKinley allegedly approached in a beige Fiat, crossed the double yellow line, pointed the vehicle toward me, accelerated, and then turned away at the last moment, missing me by about a foot. I believed I was about to be struck. I called 911. HPD report number provided to me: **22-353421**.

### 8. September 16, 2022 — TRO submitted at Wahiawā Police Station

I submitted a TRO / injunction-related filing against McKinley through Wahiawā Police Station. I retained a photo or related proof of submission.

### 9. September 19, 2022, about 12:40 p.m. — Nalu, Haleʻiwa

I observed McKinley outside Nalu before I believed service had been completed. I began recording video. He allegedly said, “that’s not necessary,” and “stop harassing me, sir.” I gathered my belongings and left. I viewed this as part of the broader pattern.

### 10. September 20, 2022, about 8:00 a.m. — Reported service / related written statement

I was informed by an HPD beat officer that McKinley had already been served. Around this period, I state that McKinley made a written defamatory remark and another remark I considered significant to the broader situation.

### 11. September 20, 2022, about 4:00 p.m. — Harassing statement, outside Breakers / North Shore Marketplace

While I was walking on foot, McKinley allegedly yelled from a rolled-down vehicle window, “How’s your parents???” I had previously told him both of my parents were deceased. I called 911. HPD report number provided to me: **22-365099**. During the police response, I was informed there had been a mix-up and that service had not yet actually been completed.

### 12. September 21, 2022, about 7:18 a.m. — Harassing drive-by, Waialua Beach Road

While I was walking east on the bike path, McKinley allegedly drove by westbound in the beige Fiat and yelled something unintelligible from the driver’s window. I called 911 and asked that the incident be added to the prior report.

### 13. September 22, 2022, about 1:29 p.m. — Harassing drive-by, Haleʻiwa Road near Haleʻiwa Beach Park

After changing my walking routine based on HPD safety advice, I was walking on Haleʻiwa Road when McKinley allegedly drove by and yelled, “Stop harassing me.” I called 911 and asked that this be added to the prior report.
